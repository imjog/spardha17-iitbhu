About Us

About Us

Why all the hype around Spardha?

For starters, 2017 will witness the 33rd edition of Spardha, a festival which has grown from strength to strength ever since its inception and now has become one of the largest and most awaited sports festivals in India.



What makes Spardha the talk of the town?

Each year thousands of participants at Spardha put their hard work to test in a vast array of sports from basketball to boxing to squash and many more. But even if this doesn’t catch your eye, the bustle of 35,000 people on the campus streets surely will. Its them who make Spardha the festival it is, when they come to cheer for their colleges in the heat or in the cold, day or night for all the four days. When that many people come and watch you play, why wouldn’t you feel like a superstar here?



What makes Spardha the talk of the town?

You take back things more golden than the medals themselves, memories. Here, memories are created anywhere and everywhere; when you win your race, when you simply lose your voice cheering for your team or when you have that ‘on top of the world’ feeling in a hot air balloon at sunset. We at Spardha break our backs with only one goal in mind, that when you leave us, you leave with a sack in hand, filled with memories you’ll treasure for life.
